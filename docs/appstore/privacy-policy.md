# FoodMap — Privacy Policy (Draft)

_Last updated: 2026-05-30. This is a draft; replace placeholder contact details
before publishing._

## Summary

FoodMap is designed to work on your device. We do not run analytics, we do not
track you across apps or websites, and we do not sell or share your personal
data with third parties. This policy reflects the app's `PrivacyInfo.xcprivacy`
manifest: no data collected, no tracking.

## What stays on your device

The following information is stored only on your device (and in your own iCloud
backups, if you enable them) and is never sent to us or to third parties:

- Your pantry contents (products, quantities, storage locations, expiry dates)
- Your dietary preferences, diet type, and selected allergens
- Household size and notification preferences
- Meal plans and shopping lists

Your dietary preferences and allergens are treated as sensitive information and
are processed entirely on-device.

## What leaves your device

- **Product lookups:** When you scan or enter a barcode, FoodMap sends that
  barcode to the Open Food Facts service (https://world.openfoodfacts.org) to
  retrieve public product information such as the product name and brand. Only
  the barcode is sent. No personal data, dietary information, or device
  identifiers are included. Open Food Facts has its own privacy practices.

No other data leaves your device.

## Camera and photos

FoodMap uses the camera to scan barcodes and to read expiry dates. If a camera
is unavailable, you can pick a photo of an expiry date from your photo library.
Images are processed on-device to recognize text and are not uploaded anywhere.

## Notifications

If you enable expiry alerts, FoodMap schedules local notifications on your
device. These reminders are generated locally and are not sent through any
server.

## Tracking

FoodMap does not track you and contains no third-party advertising or analytics
SDKs. The app declares "no tracking" in its privacy manifest.

## Children

FoodMap is a general-purpose food organization tool and does not knowingly
collect any personal data from anyone, including children.

## Not medical advice

FoodMap helps you organize food and reduce waste. It does not provide medical,
nutritional, or health advice. Allergen settings are a convenience for filtering
and reminders and must not be relied upon for safety-critical decisions.

## Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be noted in
the app's release notes.
